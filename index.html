OUR WORKING PROCESS

We have distilled our interior design process into 4 Steps – the same steps we have been using for more than 7 years, In this steps, the designer
visits your home to gather more.

1. Strategic Planning Phase

In this phase the interior designer will study the space that needs to be worked on. The designer will then move on to understanding the people in the family, their behavior, the family culture, if there are any pets - in short, every bit of information about you and your lifestyle.
